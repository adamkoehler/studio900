Bill Chappie

Bill Chappie Studio Owner,
Master Designer,

With over 26 years of experience in the beauty industry, Bill Chappie opened elements Hair Studio in Crescent Springs, KY in November of 2008. In March of 2019, Bill along with his husband Scott, opened Studio 900 in downtown Cincinnati, OH.

Creating romantic, unique and effortless cuts that fall perfectly natural is what Bill is all about. As a master stylist, he has built a reputation for designing beautiful looks and is an integral part of both elements Hair Studio and Studio 900.

Bill's approach to each client is to bring out their individuality and what compliments them most. Bill trained and worked as an educator with Sebastian International, Matrix Essentials, as well as a member of the Stylist Counsel with Proctor and Gamble.
