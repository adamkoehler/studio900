wedding style

Whether you're a bride-to-be, bridesmaid, or simply just a spectator, deciding on the proper hairstyle for a wedding is incredibly important! One must always consider the dress, the venue (Is it outdoors? If so, what happens if it's humid, rainy, or windy?), the season and the bride's inspiration before settling on a final look. When it comes to creating that perfect look, we have a highly skilled staff of hair designers and makeup artists that are reliable, professional and extremely talented. We also have stylists that are certified in hair extensions, which can all add a world of possibilities for your special day!

Finding a hairstylist for your wedding is a very personal thing. It's one of the most important events of your life and one that will be forever captured by photos. Having the best hair and make-up will make the difference in liking the photos and loving them.

On the day of your wedding, we would like to go one step further to make it the most special day possible. We offer many exclusive options, including a Private Bridal Suite, regular & airbrush makeup as well as a full service hair and nail salon.

Wedding Checklist

Collect Pictures

Browse the Internet on sites such as Pinterest and find styles you love and create an image board to bring to your appointment. Look for hairstyles that have matching hair color to yours. The same style created on different colored hair will look very different. For example, blonde hair shows texture and definition differently than dark hair.

Save the date

Wedding season is a busy and exciting time. Stylists book up, especially on weekends, so call the salon to book both the time for your hair trial and event to ensure you get the best stylists working for you on your wedding day.

Don't forget the groom

The groom and his attendants may also want to book services such a haircut, style or brow grooming.

Come in and try the style

Bring pictures of the styles you found online, a picture of your dress and any headpiece (if any) you plan to wear to the salon for a consultation and a practice up-do/style. Seeing the finished look before your wedding day is crucial for peace of mind. The less to worry about the day of the wedding, the better. If you don't love the look you see, ask the stylist to try something else. This is your time and we are committed to making certain you love your hair.

Take Pictures

Have the stylist take pictures of the final look. It is nice to have a visual reference for the day of the wedding. This will be helpful to both you and the stylist on the day of the wedding to achieve the same look again.

A note about bridesmaids

Most likely, your wedding party will be receiving services as well. It is important they love their hair. If possible, it may be fun to bring them with you for your trial run, so they can discuss hair options before the date as well.
